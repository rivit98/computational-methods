Sover (German: Sofer) is a comune (municipality) in Trentino in the northern Italian region Trentino-Alto Adige/SÃ¼dtirol, located about 25 kilometres (16 mi) northeast of Trento.


== Geography ==
As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 921 and an area of 14.7 square kilometres (5.7 sq mi).The municipality of Sover contains the frazioni (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Montesover, Piscine, Facendi, Piazzoli, Sveseri and Settefontane.
Sover borders the following municipalities: Capriana, Valfloriana, Grauno, Grumes, Segonzano, Lona-Lases and Bedollo.
