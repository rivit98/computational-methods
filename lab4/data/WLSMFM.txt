WLSM-FM (107.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Louisville, Mississippi. The station is owned by WH Properties, Inc., and airs a variety format.The station was assigned the WLSM-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 8, 1966.
