Oyster Bed Bridge is an unincorporated rural community in the township of Lot 24, Queens County, Prince Edward Island, Canada.
Oyster Bed Bridge is located at the intersections of Route 6 and Route 7, 12 kilometres (7 mi) south of North Rustico and 20 kilometres (12 mi) north of Charlottetown in the central part of the province on the north shore.
