Nowe Żeńsko [ˈnɔvɛ ˈʐɛɲskɔ] (German: Neu-Schönfeld) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Choszczno, within Choszczno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of Choszczno and 64 km (40 mi) south-east of the regional capital Szczecin.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
The village has a population of 68.
