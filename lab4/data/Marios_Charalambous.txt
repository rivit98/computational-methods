Marios Charalambous(Greek: Μάριος Χαραλάμπους) born June 18, 1969 is a former international Cypriot football defender.
He started his career in 1990 from Apollon Limassol and he played there for ten years. Also, he played in Olympiakos Nicosia for two years and he ended his career in Enosis Neon Paralimni. He also had a brief spell with Kavala F.C. during the 1999–00 Greek Alpha Ethniki season.Charalambous made 60 appearances for the Cyprus national football team from 1991 to 2002.
