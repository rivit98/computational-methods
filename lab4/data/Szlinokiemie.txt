Szlinokiemie [ʂlinɔˈkʲɛmjɛ], (Lithuanian: Šlynakiemis), is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Puńsk, within Sejny County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland, close to the border with Lithuania. It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) south-west of Puńsk, 20 km (12 mi) north-west of Sejny, and 124 km (77 mi) north of the regional capital Białystok.
