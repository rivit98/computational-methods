Al-Machriq (English translation: The East) was a journal founded in 1898 by Jesuit and Chaldean priest Louis Cheikhô, published by Jesuit fathers of Saint Joseph University in Beirut, Lebanon. The subtitle is Revue Catholique Orientale. Sciences, Lettres, Arts. It had run through 72 volumes by 1998.
