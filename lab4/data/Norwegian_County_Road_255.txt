County Road 255 (Norwegian: Fylkesvei 255) is a 108.4-kilometer (67.4 mi) road in Oppland County, Norway. It runs from Vinstra in the municipality of Nord-Fron to Lillehammer.The road is being improved (it is being widened and a pedestrian path and bicycle path are being added) between Hovemoen and Segalstad bru and a connection is being built from Hovemoen to Storhove.Before January 1, 2010 the route was a national road. It was reclassified as a county road in line with a reform transferring such routes to the counties.
