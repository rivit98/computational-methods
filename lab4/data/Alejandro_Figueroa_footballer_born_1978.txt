Alejandro Figueroa (born 18 August 1978) is a Colombian retired footballer who last played for W Connection F.C. of the TT Pro League in Trinidad and Tobago.


== Career ==
Was signed by Ma Pau Stars in December 2009.Rejoined former club W Connection in January 2013 for the upcoming season.Put in an immaculate performance in the 2013 Trinidad and Tobago Goal Shield final, making two penalty saves in the shoot-out to deny North East Stars the trophy.
