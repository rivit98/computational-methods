Reza Ahmadi (Persian: رضا احمدی‎); is an Iranian Football Defender who currently plays for Iranian football club Foolad in the Iran Pro League.


== Club career ==


=== Foolad ===
He started his career with Foolad from youth levels. Later he joined to first team by Dragan Skočić and signed three-years contract which keeping him until 2017 at Foolad. He made debut for Foolad in 2nd fixture of 2014–15 Iran Pro League against Persepolis as a substitute for Soroush Rafiei.
