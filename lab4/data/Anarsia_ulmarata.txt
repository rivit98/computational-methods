Anarsia ulmarata is a moth in the family Gelechiidae. It was described by John David Bradley in 1961. It is found on Guadalcanal.The wingspan is about 10 mm. The forewings are sordid white diffusedly irrorated (sprinkled) with fuscous and with a sprinkling of scattered blackish scales, some forming a blackish dash in the plical fold near the middle of the cell. A similar dash is found at the end of the cell and a third between this and the termen.
