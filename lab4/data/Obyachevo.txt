Obyachevo (Russian: Объячево, Komi: Абъячой) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Priluzsky District of the Komi Republic, Russia. Population: 5,699 (2010 Census); 5,835 (2002 Census); 5,273 (1989 Census).
