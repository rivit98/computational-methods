Mazan Pad (Persian: مزن پاد‎, also Romanized as Mazan Pād; also known as Delegān-e Mazan Far, Mazan Pādak, and Mazan Pat) is a village in Negur Rural District, Dashtiari District, Chabahar County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 301, in 61 families.
