Abdollahabad-e Aran (Persian: عبداله اباداران‎, also Romanized as ‘Abdollāhābād-e Ārān) is a village in Sefiddasht Rural District, in the Central District of Aran va Bidgol County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.
