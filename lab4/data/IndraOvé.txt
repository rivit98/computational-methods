Indra Ové (born 1967) is a British film, television and stage actress.


== Career ==
Ové was born in London. She trained at the Central School of Speech and Drama. She appeared in Interview with the Vampire portraying a New Orleans prostitute that Tom Cruise's Lestat interacts with. She had a small role in The Fifth Element as a VIP stewardess that Chris Tucker's character flirts with, was in Othello (playing Bianca to Laurence Fishburne's lead) and had a small role (Ella Fontaine) in Resident Evil. Her television roles include the British children's series The Latchkey Children and The New Worst Witch, the British–German sci-fi co-production Space Island One and the British medical drama television series Holby City.  She also appeared in a 1992 episode of Desmond's, playing Samantha. She appeared in a 1994 episode of  Soldier Soldier, playing Melanie Burrows and also in a 1999 two-part Bugs story as Zephr. In 2019, she appeared as a First Order officer in Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker.She is the sister of artist/director Zak Ové and the daughter of pioneer film maker Horace Ové.
