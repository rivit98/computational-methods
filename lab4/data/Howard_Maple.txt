Howard Albert "Mape" Maple (born July 20, 1903 in Adrian, Missouri – November 9, 1970) was an NFL player for the Chicago Cardinals in 1930 and a Major League Baseball catcher for the Washington Senators in 1932.  He played for Oregon State University in college and is the only Oregon State alumni to play in both the NFL and the MLB.
