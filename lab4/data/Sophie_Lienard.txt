Sophie Lienard (active 1837-1845) was a French portrait artist who specialized in miniatures. She worked and lived in Paris. Between 1842 and 1845, she exhibited at the Salon in Paris.During her lifetime, she painted portraits of Queen Victoria, Jérôme Bonaparte and Louis Philippe I.


== Notable collections ==
Noble con abrigo negro (Noble with a black coat), ca. 1845, Museum of Romanticism, Madrid
