Irina Reyn is an American novelist. Her novel, What Happened to Anna K., was selected as the tenth best fiction book of 2008 by Jennifer Reese of Entertainment Weekly, and won the 2009 Goldberg Prize for Jewish Fiction by emerging writers.Reyn was born in Moscow, Russia. She is an assistant professor of English at the University of Pittsburgh.
