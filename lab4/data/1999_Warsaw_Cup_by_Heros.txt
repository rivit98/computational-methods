The 1999 Warsaw Cup by Heros was a tennis tournament played on outdoor clay courts in Warsaw, Poland that was part of Tier IV of the 1999 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from 3 May until 9 May 1999. Cristina Torrens Valero won the singles title.

== Finals ==


=== Singles ===

 Cristina Torrens Valero defeated  Inés Gorrochategui, 7–5, 7–6(7–3)
It was Torrens Valero's first title of the year and her career, after having reached her first final a fortnight earlier.
