American actor Jesse Eisenberg has received numerous accolades and nominations throughout his acting career. He is best known for playing Facebook founder Mark Zuckerberg in The Social Network (2010), for which he received BAFTA Award, Golden Globe, and Academy Award nominations in the Best Actor category.


== Major associations ==


=== Academy Awards ===
The Academy Awards are a set of awards given annually for excellence of cinematic achievements. The awards, organized by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences (AMPAS), were first held in 1929 at the Hollywood Roosevelt Hotel. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== British Academy Film Awards ===
The British Academy Film Award is an annual award show presented by the British Academy of Film and Television Arts. The awards were founded in 1947 as The British Film Academy, by David Lean, Alexander Korda, Carol Reed, Charles Laughton, Roger Manvell and others. Eisenberg has received two nominations, for the BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role and the BAFTA Rising Star Award.


=== Golden Globe Awards ===
The Golden Globe Award is an accolade bestowed by the 93 members of the Hollywood Foreign Press Association (HFPA) recognising excellence in film and television, both domestic and foreign. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== Screen Actors Guild Awards ===
The Screen Actors Guild Awards are organised by the Screen Actors Guild‐American Federation of Television and Radio Artists. First awarded in 1995, the awards aim to recognise excellent achievements in film and television.[14] Eisenberg has received two nominations.


== Film critics awards ==


=== Broadcast Film Critics Association ===
The Broadcast Film Critics Association (BFCA), founded in 1995, is the largest film critics organisation in the United States and Canada.[1] The BFCA has presented the Critics' Choice Movie Awards each year since 1995. Eisenberg has received two nominations.


=== Chicago Film Critics Association ===
The Chicago Film Critics Association (CFCA) is an association of professional film critics, who work in print, broadcast and online media, based in Chicago, Illinois, United States.[1] Since 1989, the CFCA has given out annual awards that recognise the best films in a variety of categories. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== Dallas–Fort Worth Film Critics Association ===
The Dallas–Fort Worth Film Critics Association is an organisation of radio, television, and internet journalists from Dallas–Fort Worth-based publications. Eisenberg has received one 3rd-place from two nominations.


=== IndieWire Critics Poll ===
The IndieWire Critics Poll is a poll to recognize the best in American and international films. Eisenberg has received runner-up from one nomination.


=== London Film Critics' Circle ===
The London Film Critics' Circle, founded in 1913, is an association for working British critics. Eisenberg has received one nomination, for Actor of the Year.


=== National Society of Film Critics ===
The National Society of Film Critics is a film critic organisation, founded in 1966. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination.


=== Online Film Critics Society ===
The Online Film Critics Society (OFCS), founded in 1997, is an association of online film. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== San Diego Film Critics Society ===
The San Diego Film Critics Society (SDFCS), an organisation of film reviewers in San Diego, was founded in 1996 to award films. Eisenberg has received two nominations.


=== St. Louis Film Critics Association ===
The St. Louis Film Critics Association, founded in 2004, is an organization of film critics operating in Greater St. Louis. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== Toronto Film Critics Association ===
The Toronto Film Critics Association (TFCA) is an organisation of film reviewers from Toronto-based publications. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination.


=== Vancouver Film Critics Circle ===
The Vancouver Film Critics Circle (VFCC) was founded by David Spaner and Ian Caddell in 2000. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


=== Village Voice Film poll ===
The Village Voice Film Poll (VVFP) is an annual polling by The Village Voice film section of more than 100 major film critics for alternative media sources. Eisenberg has received one award for one nomination.


=== Washington D.C. Area Film Critics Association ===
The Washington D.C. Area Film Critics Association (WAFCA)s a group of film critics in Washington, founded in 2002. Eisenberg has received one nomination.


== Film festival awards ==


=== Abu Dhabi Film Festival ===
The Abu Dhabi Film Festival (ADFF) is an international film festival, started in 2007. The ADFF aims to showcase the best films from the region alongside standout productions from prominent international filmmakers. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination in the New Horizons Competition.


=== Big Apple Film Festival ===
The Big Apple Film Festival (BAFF) takes place annually. It is dedicated to "showcasing and promoting the highest quality films from the New York City independent film community, as well as additional specially selected films from across the country and around the world." Each year BAFF presents awards in a variety of categories including Best Feature Film, Short Film, Documentary, Animation, Experimental, NYC Cityscape Award, Student Film Award, Golden Apple Award, and NY Emerging Talent Award. Eisenberg received one award, the NY Emerging Talent Award, from one nomination.


=== Film Club's The Lost Weekend ===
Film Club's The Lost Weekend is an annual celebration of film at the Alamo Drafthouse Cinema featuring eight films from around the world. Eisenberg received one award from one nomination.


=== Palm Springs International Film Festival ===
Founded in 1989 in Palm Springs, California, the Palm Springs International Film Festival is held annually in January. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination.


=== San Diego Film Festival ===
The San Diego Festival is a five-day independent film festival that takes place annually in San Diego and is produced by the non-profit San Diego Film Foundation. Held in October in San Diego's Gaslamp Quarter and La Jolla, the festival is a showcase for US and international independent filmmakers. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination.


=== Vail Film Festival ===
The Vail Film Festival is a four-day film festival that has taken place annually in late March or early April in Vail, Colorado since 2004. Eisenberg has received one award from one nomination, for the Rising Star Award.
