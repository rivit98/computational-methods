Krasny Bor (Russian: Красный Бор) is a rural locality (a settlement) in Kalininskoye Rural Settlement, Tarnogsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 384 as of 2002. There are 6 streets.


== Geography ==
The distance to Totma is 43 km, to Tsaryova is 18 km. Isayevo is the nearest rural locality.
