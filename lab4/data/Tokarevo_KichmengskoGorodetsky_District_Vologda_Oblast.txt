Tokarevo (Russian: Токарево) is a rural locality (a village) in Gorodetskoye Rural Settlement, Kichmengsko-Gorodetsky District, Vologda Oblast, Russia. The population was 68 as of 2002. There are 2 streets.


== Geography ==
The distance to Kichmengsky Gorodok is 19 km. Zhevnino is the nearest rural locality.
