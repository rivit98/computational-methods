Morchella eohespera is a species of fungus in the Morchellaceae family described as new to science in 2016. In North America, it has been collected from Newfoundland and Labrador, New Brunswick and Washington State. It is also found in central and northern Europe, and central China. It is in the Morchella elata clade.
