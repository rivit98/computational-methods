Arutela was a fort in the Roman province of Dacia located on Limes Alutanus. It was erected on 138 by "Surri sagittari" by the order of Titus Flavius Constans, imperial procurator of Dacia Inferior.The last Roman coin found here was from Elagabal, emitted between 220 - 222.
