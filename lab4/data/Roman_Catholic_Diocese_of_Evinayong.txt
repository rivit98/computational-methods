The Roman Catholic Diocese of Evinayong is a Latin Catholic suffragan bishopric in the ecclesiastical province (covering all Equatorial Guinea) of the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Malabo, yet depends on the missionary Roman Congregation for the Evangelization of Peoples.
Its cathedral episcopal see is the Catedral de San José, dedicated to Saint Joseph, in Evinayong, capital of Centro Sur province, Región Continental.
No statistics available.


== History ==
Established in 2017.04.01 as Diocese of Evinayong (Spanish) / Evinayongen(sis) (Latin), on territory split off from the Diocese of Bata (in the same ecclesiastical province).
