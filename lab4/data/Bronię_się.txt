"Bronię się" (English: I'm defending myself) is a song by Polish singer Sarsa, and the lead single off of her second studio album Pióropusze. The song was released in Poland as a digital download on 10 March 2017, through Universal Music Polska. It was written by Sarsa Markiewicz, and produced by Markiewicz along with Steve Manovski.After its release, the song reached number-three on the Polish Airplay – New Chart, and number-26 on the Polish Airplay chart.


== Music video ==
A music video to accompany the release of "Bronię się" was released on 9 March 2017 through Sarsa's Vevo channel. It was directed by Marcin Starzecki.
