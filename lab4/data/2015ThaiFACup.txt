The Chang FA Cup 2015 (Thai: ช้าง เอฟเอคัพ) is the 20th season of Thailand knockout football competition. The tournament is organized by the Football Association of Thailand.
The cup winner is guaranteed a place in the  2016 AFC Champions League Play-off.
