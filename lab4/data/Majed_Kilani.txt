Majed Kilani (born 1 January 1997) is a Tunisian tennis player.
Kilani has a career high ATP singles ranking of 921 achieved on 1 August 2016. He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 621, also achieved on 6 January 2020. Kilani has won 3 ITF doubles titles. 
Kilani was studied at University of Tulsa, between 2015-2019.Kilani has represented Tunisia at Davis Cup. In Davis Cup he has a win-loss record of 4-0.
