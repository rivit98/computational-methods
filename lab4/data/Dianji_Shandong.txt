Dianji (Chinese: 店集; pinyin: Diànjí) is a former town in eastern Shandong province, China, located around 24 kilometres (15 mi) northeast of Jimo, which administers it. In 2012, as part of an administrative restructuring, it was merged into the neighboring town of Jinkou, Shandong.
