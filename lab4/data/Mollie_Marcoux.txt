Mollie Marcoux is the current director of athletics for Princeton University. She previously served for nineteen years as an executive for Chelsea Piers Management in Connecticut, prior to beginning her career in college athletic administration. Marcoux attended college at Princeton University, where she played on the school's women's ice hockey and soccer teams. Marcoux was named athletic director at Princeton University on April 15, 2014.
