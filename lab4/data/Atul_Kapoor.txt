Atul Kapoor (Hindi: अतुल कपूर, Atula Kapūra, born 28 December 1966 in Mumbai) is an Indian actor and voice actor who can speak English and Hindi.
He mostly has worked with dubbing foreign content into the Hindi language as part of his voice-acting career. He started off in 2002 before he made a big breakthrough in his career by hosting Bigg Boss on Sony in 2006 and on colors from 2008.
