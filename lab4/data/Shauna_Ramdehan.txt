Shauna Ramdehan (born 1989 in Georgetown, Guyana) is a Guyanese beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Guyana 2015 and represented her country at Miss Universe 2015 in Las Vegas, United States.


== Personal life ==
Ramdehan lives in Georgetown, Guyana and works as a Foreign Services Officer in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. On 16 November 2015 Ramdehan was crowned Miss Universe Guyana 2015. As Miss Guyana, Ramdehan competed at Miss Universe 2015, representing Guyana.
