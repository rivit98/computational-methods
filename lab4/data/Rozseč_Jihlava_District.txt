Rozseč (Czech pronunciation: [ˈros.sɛtʃ]) is a village and municipality (obec) in Jihlava District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of 8.27 square kilometres (3.19 sq mi), and has a population of 198 (as at 28 August 2006).
Rozseč lies approximately 27 kilometres (17 mi) south of Jihlava and 135 km (84 mi) south-east of Prague.
