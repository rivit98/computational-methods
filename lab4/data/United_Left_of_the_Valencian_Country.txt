United Left of the Valencian Country (Catalan: Esquerra Unida del País Valencià) is the Valencian federation of the Spanish left wing political and social movement United Left. Marga Sanz is the current General Coordinator and Ignacio Blanco Giner its spokesperson.
The Communist Party of the Valencian Country (PCPV, Valencian federation of PCE) is the major member of the coalition.
