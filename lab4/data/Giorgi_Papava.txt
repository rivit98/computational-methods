Giorgi Papava (born 16 February 1993) is a Georgian football player. He is a midfielder and plays for FC Dinamo Tbilisi and the Georgia national football team. He is 186 cm tall and weighs 71 kg. 


== Honours ==
Dinamo TbilisiGeorgian League: 2012–13; 2013–14
Georgian Cup: 2013 ; 2014
Georgian Super Cup: 2014
