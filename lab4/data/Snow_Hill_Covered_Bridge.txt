Snow Hill Covered Bridge, also known as Johnson Fork Covered Bridge, is a historic Howe Truss covered bridge located in Whitewater Township, Franklin County, Indiana.  The bridge was built in 1895, and measures 75 feet long.  It has a gable roof, is clad in board and natten siding, and has an open clerestory.It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1995.
