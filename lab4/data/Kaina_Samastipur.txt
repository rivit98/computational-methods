Kaina is a village in Singhia block in Rosera Subdivision in Samastipur District in the state of Bihar, India. Kaina has 5 localities named Panchkhuttee, Tinkhuttee, Pachwair toal, Tola par and Puwari Tol. Its population is around 2331.
Kaina is mostly a village of Brahmins(maithil), 90% of the population have the surname Jha. 4 ponds and 4 temples are situated in the village. Literacy level is also good in the village.
It is one of the most backward villages of Samastipur district. Open defecation is still a common sight here. Untouchabiliy of lower castes is still maintained here.
