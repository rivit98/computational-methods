Songshan (Chinese: 松山; pinyin: Sōngshān) is a town of Bairi (Tenzhu) Tibetan Autonomous County in southeastern Gansu province, China, located 13 km (8.1 mi) due east of the county seat. As of 2011, it has one residential community (社区) and 15 villages under its administration.
