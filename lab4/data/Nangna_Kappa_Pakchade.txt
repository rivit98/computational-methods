Nangna Kappa Pakchade (English: Tears of a Woman) is a 2013 Manipur film directed by Mankhonmani Mongsaba and produced by Thoungamba Thouyangba, under the banner of PK Films. The film features Leishangthem Tonthoi and Denny Likmabam in the lead roles, with Sorojini, Leibakshemba, L. Imo, Lourembam Pishak and many others portraying supporting roles. The story of the film was written by Maharaj Kumari Binodini Devi. The film got selection in the 3rd Delhi International Film Festival 2014.  The film was screened at Gorky Sadan Hall, Kolkata on 8 March 2014 on the occasion of International  Women's Day. It also got selection at Ladakh International Film Festival 2014 in the Indian Feature section.

== About ==
This film is the fifth production of PK Films. It narrates the story of a woman Nungshitombi, who fought against her husband with courage by filing a case in the court, where her husband denies Nungshitombi and her child as his own wife and his child. It is a 35mm celluloid film.
