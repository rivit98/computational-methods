The European Volleyball Championship of the Small Countries Division is a biannual sport competition for men, women and junior national teams. It is recognized and fully supported by the European Volleyball Confederation (CEV) and organized by one of its zonal associations called CEV Small Countries Division (CEV SCD). Only national federations of the 15 countries which are members of the CEV SCD are eligible to participate in the tournaments.
