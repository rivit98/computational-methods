Plicisyrinx vitjazi is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Pseudomelatomidae, the turrids and allies.


== Description ==


== Distribution ==
This marine species was found in the Kurile-Kamchatka Trench, Northern Pacific Ocean.
