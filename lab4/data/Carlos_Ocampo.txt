Carlos Cesar Ocampo Manriquez (born 9 November 1995) is a Mexican professional boxer who challenged for the IBF welterweight title in 2018.
As an amateur, he won silver medals in the 2009 and 2011 Mexican National Olympiads.He made his professional debut on July 2012. On July 2017, the IBF ordered a title eliminator between Ocampo and Konstantin Ponomarev. The winner will be the mandatory challenger to IBF champion Errol Spence Jr. The fight was later called off as Ponomarev pulled out for undisclosed reasons.
