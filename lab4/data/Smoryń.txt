Smoryń [ˈsmɔrɨɲ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Frampol, within Biłgoraj County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) east of Frampol, 14 km (9 mi) north of Biłgoraj, and 67 km (42 mi) south of the regional capital Lublin. The village has a population of 299.
