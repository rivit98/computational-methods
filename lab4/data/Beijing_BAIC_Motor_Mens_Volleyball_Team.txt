Beijing BAIC Motor Men’s Volleyball Club (Chinese: 北京汽車男排), is a Chinese men’s volleyball club based in Beijing, founded in 1950s and changed to a professional club in 2010.It is established by Beijing Municipal Sports Bureau and BAIC Group on September 14, 2010. The team currently plays in the Chinese Men's League. They won the champions twice.
