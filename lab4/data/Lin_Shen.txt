Lin Shen or Lin Yushen (Chinese: 林雨申; born October 23, 1980) is a Chinese actor. His mother was the film producer Li Xiaowan. He first appeared in 2012 in the film Watch Sky, and received media attention. In 2019, he was an actor for the television series Heavenly Sword and Dragon Slaying Sabre. 


== Awards ==
In 2013, he was awarded the award for the Most Potentious Male Actor for the 16th Shanghai Movie Festival.
