J. Teddy Garces (born September 12, 1974) is an American film, television and stage actor, best known for his portrayal of Bruiser in the web series The Guild and Julian in Ted Sampon: Househusband.  He was born Joseph Teddy Garces at St. Lukes Hospital in Manhattan, New York City, to a Dominican father Joseph Garces and a Dominican mother (internationally recognized poet) Yvelisse Fanith.In 2011 Teddy filmed the first few episodes of his web series Sound Advice On The Web in which he casts, writes and plays in.
