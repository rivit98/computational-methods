KZLL-LD is a digital low-powered television station that is licensed to Joplin, Missouri, but has since relocated to Tulsa, Oklahoma. It is a Country Network affiliate owned by Sunrise, Florida-based DTV America Corporation. The station's digital signal is broadcast over UHF channel 24.


== Digital television ==
The station's digital signal is multi-plexed.
