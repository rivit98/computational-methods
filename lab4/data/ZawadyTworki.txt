Zawady-Tworki [zaˈvadɨ ˈtfɔrki] (German: Sawadden, 1938-45: Grenzwacht) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Prostki, within Ełk County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) east of Prostki, 19 km (12 mi) south-east of Ełk, and 139 km (86 mi) east of the regional capital Olsztyn.
Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (East Prussia).
The village has a population of 140.
