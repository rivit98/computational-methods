Edward M. Early (born October 2, 1935) is a former member of the Pennsylvania State Senate, serving from 1975 to 1986.  He also served in the Pennsylvania House of Representatives.


== Life ==
Born on October 2, 1935 in Allegheny County, Pennsylvania, Edward M. Early was a son of Edward A. Early and Christina Marie (Snead) Early, and was married to Sandy Chestnut.A Democrat, he was a member of the Pennsylvania State House of Representatives from 1971 to 1974, and then represented the 40th District of the Pennsylvania State Senate from 1975 to 1986.
