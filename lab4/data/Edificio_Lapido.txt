Edificio Lapido is a building on the 18 de Julio Avenue in Centro, Montevideo, Uruguay.
It is a multifunctional building, built in 1933 and designed by architect Juan Aubriot. It is a fine example of the Uruguayan international architectural renewal of the early decades of the 20th century. It is a National Heritage Site since 1989.
