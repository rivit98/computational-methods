Laneville is an unincorporated community in south central Rusk County, Texas, United States.


== History ==
The town came into being as a crossroads community in the 1880s. The intersection of four "lanes" furnished the town's name. The town was granted a post office in 1888.  Today, the "four" lanes are FM 225 and FM 1798


== Education ==
The community of Laneville is served by the Laneville Independent School District and home to the Laneville High School Yellow Jackets.
