Innocent Wafula (born 1 April 1996) is a Kenyan  footballer who plays as a Midfielder for Vipers SC in the Ugandan Premier League.


== Career ==


=== Chemelil Sugar Football Club ===
Innocent joined Chemelil Sugar F.C in 2014 and made his debut against Muhoroni Youth on 22 February 2014. Chemelil Sugar Football Club won 2-0, Innocent made both assists. He scored his first goal for Chemelil Sugar Football Club against Tusker F.C on 23 April 2014 at Ruaraka Stadium Nairobi.


=== GorMahia ===
He joined Gor Mahia  in 2015 from Chemelil Sugar Football Club.  Innocent on his debut for Gor Mahia against KCB he scored and the match ended 1-0. In January 2017 he signed a new two-year contract deal at Gor Mahia.  On 17 September 2017, Innocent scored a memorable goal for Gor Mahia against Sony Sugar at Nyayo National Stadium and Gor Mahia unprecedented the 15th Kenyan Premier League title thus taking it for the third successive year.


=== Vipers S.C ===
Innocent joined Vipers SC In January 2019 and signed a three-year deal.
