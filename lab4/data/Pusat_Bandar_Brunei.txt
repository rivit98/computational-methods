Pusat Bandar (Malay for City Centre) or simply Bandar (literally means City) is the city centre of Bandar Seri Begawan, the capital of Brunei. It is home to a number of important government offices, commercial and financial establishments, as well as national landmarks. Pusat Bandar is officially a subdivision of Kianggeh, a mukim or subdistrict of Brunei-Muara, with the postcode BS8611.
