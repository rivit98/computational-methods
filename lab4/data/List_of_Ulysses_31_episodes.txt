Ulysses 31 is a Japanese-French animated science fiction television series produced by DiC Entertainment. The show comprises 26 half-hour episodes which first aired between 1981 and 1982 on FR3. The following episode titles were used for the original French version as well as in the international version for Europe and North and Latin America. The order of episodes on the DVD release is different however.
