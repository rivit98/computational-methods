Sulyman Age Abdulkareem is a professor of chemical engineering and former Vice-Chancellor of Al-Hikmah University in Ilorin, Nigeria.. Now, appointed as the new Vice-Chancellor of University of Ilorin on 28, August 2017. Council approved the appointment of Professor Sulyman Age Abdulkareem as the 10th Vice Chancellor of the University of Ilorin. The appointment is to take effect from 16 October, 2017. He was preceded by Abdul Ganiyu Ambali.
He graduated from University of Detroit in 1980 for BChE and MChE (Chemical Engineering); and University of Louisville, in 1988 for Ph.D. in (Chem. Eng.). His area of specialization is Heterogeneous Catalysis/Reaction Engineering.
