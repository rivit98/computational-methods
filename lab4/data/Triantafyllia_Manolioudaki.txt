Triantafyllia Manolioudaki (Greek: Τριανταφυλλιά Μανωλιουδάκη; born 19 March 1986) is a Greek water polo player, captain of the Greek powerhouse Olympiacos and key member of the Greek national team.
Manolioudaki was part of the Greek national team awarded the gold medal at the 2011 World Aquatics Championships which took place in Shanghai in July 2011.
