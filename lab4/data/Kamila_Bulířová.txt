Kamila Bulířová  (born June 7, 1977) is a Czech extreme sports athlete and member of the Direct Alpine Team.
Bulířová is also member of the national ski mountaineering team, she takes part in triathlons and mountain biking competitions.


== Selected results ==


=== Ski mountaineering ===
2003:
10th, European Championship team race (together with Lucie Oršulová)
2004:
4th, World Championship relay race (together with Alice Korbová and Lucie Oršulová)
