Nutrimato is a trademark of the Mott's company which denotes a drink made primarily of reconstituted tomato juice concentrate and seasoned beef broth, with added vitamins and iron. Nutrimato was produced beginning in 1972 by the Duffy-Mott company in California and discontinued in 1975.
Nutrimato was one of the "Duffy-Mato" line of blended juices, comprising Clamato, Beefamato and Nutrimato.
