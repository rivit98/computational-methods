Sadaa Mee Sevalo (English translation: Always at Your Service) is a 2005 Telugu movie directed by G. Neelakanta Reddy starring Shriya and  Venu Thottempudi.


== Plot ==
Tilak (Venu) is a mind games expert and tries to help people in need. He, his friend (Sunil) who is a lawyer, and a former police officer (Mallikarjuna Rao) help needy people who are in the public. Venu falls in love with Suryakantham (Shriya Saran). He follows her and they both fall in love. Suryakantham has difficulty adjusting to help people in need. Suryakantham gets kidnapped by Tilak's enemies. The rest of the movie is about how Tilak saves her.
